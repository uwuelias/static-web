How to Cook Instant Noodle?

  1. Get your instant noodle and egg
  2. Boil water in a pot and wait till boil
  3. Add seasoning packet(s) and noodles in
  4. Add egg in
  5. Let the noodle cook for 2-3 minutes (remember to stir occasionally)
  6. EAT
